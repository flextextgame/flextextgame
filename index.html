Game

Trove is a MMORPG ( Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game) survival based game with a similar concept to Minecraft. Although Trove allows players to build structures, mine blocks, use blockish characters, and play in a survival based environment, this focus more on the rpg style. This game features eight classes, pets, unique equipments, and great fighting animations.
